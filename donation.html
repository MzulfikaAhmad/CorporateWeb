Safe giving process

Verify before you transfer

Check the official domain

Use the official portal The verified PERKIM Selangor donation website uses the infaq.perkimselangor.org.my domain. linked from PERKIM Selangor channels.

Read the payment summary

Confirm the donation amount, transaction charge and payment method before continuing to FPX.

Protect personal data

Do not send identification documents, passwords or banking credentials through unverified messages.

Keep your receipt

Save the official confirmation for personal records and future enquiries.
